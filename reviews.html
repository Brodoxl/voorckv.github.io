WHAT THEY SAY

A blistering assault on the senses and the establishment. The Function channels pure, unadulterated punk fury into complex, thought-provoking art-rock. Essential listening for the disillusioned.

Johnny Rotten-Soundalike - Punk Chronicle Weekly

Forget your polished pop drivel. This is the real deal – raw, intelligent, and unapologetically loud. Their live show is a chaotic masterpiece.

Sid Vicious' Ghost - Anarchy Music Mag

Confusing rhythms? Check. Political rage? Check. Élise Pommet's vocals could strip paint. 'Riot, Riot, Riot' is the soundtrack to the revolution we need.

Angry Critic Mcgee - The Daily Noise

While drawing from punk legends, The Function carves its own path. It's Bowie meets The Clash in a dark alley, and the result is thrillingly unpredictable.

Art School Dropout - Avant-Garde Times

Saw them live last week. My ears are still ringing, and my perspective on the monarchy is forever changed. Go see them. Now.

Random Fan - Live Show Review Blog

Not for the faint of heart, or for fans of predictable three-chord punk. The Function demands attention and rewards it with layers of sonic intensity and lyrical depth.

Serious Music Person - Rock Intellectual Monthly
